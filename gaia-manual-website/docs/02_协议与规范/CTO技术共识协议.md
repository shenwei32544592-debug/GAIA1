# CTO技术共识协议
Chief Technology Officer Consensus Protocol  
**适用范围**：全体技术负责人及工程合作方  
**协议性质**：具有约束力的运营规约

## 1. 核心共识
首席技术官（CTO）需明确signa.ink的核心定位：  
**“非设计平台，而是实体身份标识基础设施。”**

## 2. 工程体系定位
工程体系的核心职能为**约束编译系统**，而非创作主体。  
其核心职责包括：  
● 检测实体落地的不可行性  
● 完整留存创作数据  
● 保障落地执行的完整性  
● **落实任何节点提出的 IT 建设需求**：负责对接与承接全系节点与时俱进的 IT 需求，保障联盟高效、先进

## 3. 明确禁止行为
工程体系严禁实施以下操作：  
● 生成艺术创作路径  
● 基于贡献者数据开展模型训练  
● 无追溯记录修改原始矢量文件  
● 进行无提示的自动修正操作

## 4. 版本完整性要求
所有标识的迭代版本均需完整留存以下内容：  
● 原始版本  
● 修改版本  
● 完整的差异追溯记录  
**禁止对任何版本进行覆盖操作。**

## 5. 数据主权原则
艺术创作源文件遵循以下规则：  
● 全程加密存储  
● 访问权限严格管控  
● 不设置任何二次使用权

## 6. 合规声明
首席技术官（CTO）需作出如下正式确认：  
**“工程工作须以守护人类创作主导性为核心，即便为此牺牲操作便捷性或执行效率。”**

## 7. 分配参数：不可改权与设计依据
- 分配引擎参数（65/5/20/10、MMG、创世公民机制、FCR、公益金相关）由**创始人锁定**，CTO 及执行节点无权修改；变更须经创始人书面同意及约定多签程序
- 创始人须在系统上线前，向技术负责人提供《财务分配参数设计依据》文档；技术负责人有权提出澄清请求
- 设计依据文档供实现 fidelity、审计追溯及交接使用；技术负责人理解参数合理性后实施，但不得据此主张变更权

# ③《CTO 技术共识协议》
**Chief Technology Officer Consensus Protocol**

**Applies To:** All Technical Leads & Engineering Partners  
**Nature:** Binding Operational Covenant

---

## 1. Core Acknowledgement
The CTO acknowledges that signa.ink is:

“Not a design platform, but a **physical identity infrastructure**.”

---

## 2. Engineering Role Definition
The engineering system functions as a **constraint compiler**, not a creative agent.

Its responsibility is to:

+ Detect physical infeasibility
+ Preserve authorial data
+ Maintain execution integrity

---

## 3. Explicit Prohibitions
The system must not:

+ Generate artistic paths
+ Train on contributor data
+ Modify original vectors without trace
+ Perform silent corrections

---

## 4. Version Integrity
All mark iterations must preserve:

+ Original version
+ Modified version
+ Full diff trace

No overwrite is permitted.

---

## 5. Data Sovereignty
+ Artistic source files are encrypted
+ Access is strictly permissioned
+ No secondary usage rights exist

---

## 6. Compliance Statement
The CTO affirms:

“Engineering shall protect authorship even at the cost of convenience or speed.”

---

## 7. Allocation Parameters: Non-Amendment & Design Rationale
- Distribution engine parameters (65/5/20/10, MMG, Batch 0 mechanics, FCR, public good fund) are **Founder-locked**; CTO and execution nodes have no amendment authority; changes require Founder's written consent and agreed multi-signatory process
- Founder shall provide the *Financial Allocation Parameters – Design Rationale* document to the technical lead before system launch; the technical lead may request clarification
- The rationale document serves implementation fidelity, audit traceability, and handover; the technical lead implements upon understanding the rationale but has no right to claim amendment authority on that basis

---

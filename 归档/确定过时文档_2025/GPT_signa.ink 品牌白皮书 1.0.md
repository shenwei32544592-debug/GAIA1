## 01｜我们看到的时代
### Context: The Crisis of Identity in the Pixel Age

**CN**

在数字复制几乎零成本的时代，  
我们正身处一个悖论之中：  
交流效率前所未有，  
而身份的真实感却在消退。

一个人的存在，  
正在被算法、模板与平台不断稀释。

而名字，  
是人类身份最具代表性的象征——  
一种世代相传的宣誓。

亲笔签下，  
即承载承诺、责任与法律人格。

我们拒绝让它被标准字体统一，  
或被算法随意生成；  
我们选择亲自定义——  
由人类设计师，  
按本人意愿完成。

名字，  
是人所剩不多的个性印记。  
也是主权的最后边界。

signa.ink 的诞生，  
不是为了制造更多视觉符号，  
而是为了重新面对一个被长期悬置的问题：

在这个时代，  
一个人的印记，  
是否还值得被认真对待？

**EN**

In an era where digital reproduction approaches zero cost,  
we find ourselves in a paradox:  
unprecedented communication efficiency,  
at the expense of tangible identity.

Personal presence is steadily diluted  
by algorithms, templates, and platforms.

Yet the name remains humanity's most potent symbol of identity—  
an ancestral oath passed down through generations.

To sign it by hand is to bear commitment, responsibility, and legal personhood.

We refuse to have it standardized by fonts  
or arbitrarily generated by algorithms;  
we choose to define it ourselves—  
through human designers  
who follow our will.

The name is among the few personal marks left to us.  
It is also the last frontier of sovereignty.

signa.ink was not created to produce more visual symbols,  
but to confront a long-suspended question:

In this age,  
does a person's mark  
still deserve to be taken seriously?

## 02｜我们的区分：印记不是签名
### We Do Not Design Signatures

**CN**

签名是一种行为，  
印记是一种身份。

signa.ink 不做装饰，  
也不追流行。  
我们关心的是：  
当你的名字被刻入材质、得以留存时，  
它是否真正代表你。

因此，我们不称他们为「用户」，  
而称其为 **定义者**。

印记一旦被定义，  
便不再是商品，  
而是你的身份。

**EN**

A signature is an action.  
A mark is an identity.

signa.ink does not pursue decoration  
nor follow trends.  
We care about one thing:  
when your name is embedded in material and preserved,  
does it truly represent you.

That is why we do not call them "users,"  
but **Definers**.

Once a mark is defined,  
it is no longer a product—  
it is your identity.

## 03｜认证：信任的物理化
### Certification: Trust Made Tangible

**CN**

在 signa.ink，  
认证不是装饰，而是结构的一部分。

认证的意义，  
不在于证明我们是谁，  
而在于确认：

**这一枚印记，只属于你。**

借助认证卡与唯一身份编号，  
为每一枚印记构建可追溯的信任基础。

**EN**

At signa.ink,  
certification is not decoration—it is structure.

Its purpose is not to validate who we are,  
but to confirm one thing clearly:

**This mark belongs to you alone.**

Through certification cards and unique identity sequences,  
each mark receives a traceable foundation of trust.
